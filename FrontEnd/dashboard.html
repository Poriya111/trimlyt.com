Dashboard Guide

This is your main command center.

• Revenue (Big Number): Cash secured. Only counts 'Finished' appointments.
• Exp (Expected): Your potential total. Counts both 'Finished' and 'Scheduled' appointments.
• Goal: Tracks your progress toward your monthly target based on actual revenue.
• Walk-in: Quickly logs a cut as 'Finished' for right now, skipping the schedule.
