Dashboard Guide

This page is your control center. It summarizes revenue, service performance, and busy hours so you can manage your business efficiently.

Key Sections

  • Revenue Cards: The large number shows confirmed revenue (Finished appointments). The expected value includes both Finished and Scheduled appointments.
  • Goal: Shows progress toward your monthly revenue target. Update this in Settings.
  • Busy Hours: Visualizes when you typically have appointments—useful for staffing and promotions.
  • Service Performance: See which services bring the most revenue so you can optimize your offerings.

How Google Integration Works

If you connect a Google account, Trimlyt can import events from your Google Calendar. Only events you explicitly mark for Trimlyt are imported — use the TL [Service] [Price] format in the event title (example: TL Haircut 25).

Important: Trimlyt does not access your email or send messages on your behalf. We only request minimal permissions to read and write calendar events and see your basic account email.

Best Practices

  • Create appointments directly in Trimlyt when possible — this ensures accurate metrics and mobile UX.
  • Use Google import for bulk or historic events only; mark events with the TL prefix to avoid accidental imports.
  • Set a sensible Appointment Gap in Settings to prevent overlapping bookings.
