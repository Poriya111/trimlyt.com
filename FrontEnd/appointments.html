Appointments Guide

Manage your schedule and history from here. This modal explains the list, actions, and how integration behaves so there are no surprises.

List & Search

Use the search box to filter by service name, extras, or client email. Upcoming appointments are shown first; past appointments appear under "Past".

Actions

  • Edit: Change time, service, or details. Edits sync to Google Calendar if connected.
  • Finish: Marks the appointment as completed and counts toward revenue.
  • No Show / Cancel: Updates status; canceled/no-show appointments will be removed from Google Calendar when applicable.
  • Delete: Removes appointment from Trimlyt and Google Calendar (if previously synced).

Adding Appointments

Use + Add to create appointments directly in Trimlyt for best accuracy. For quick in-person jobs use Walk-in, which creates a finished appointment immediately.

Google Calendar Integration

When you choose to import from Google Calendar, Trimlyt only imports events you explicitly mark with the TL [Service] [Price] format in the event title (example: TL Haircut 25). This prevents accidental imports of personal events.

Note: Trimlyt does not access or send your emails. Import and sync are limited to calendar events and basic identity information.
