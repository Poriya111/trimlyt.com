Settings Guide

Configure Trimlyt to match your workflow. Below are the main settings and how they affect the app.

Integrations

Connect your Google account to import calendar events (TL format). We only request minimal permissions: read/write calendar events and your basic account email. We do not access or send your email.

Monthly Goal

Set your target revenue for the month. The Dashboard progress bar uses this value.

Services

Manage the list of services you offer. These populate the appointment creation form and help with quick entry and analytics.

Appointment Gap

This enforces a minimum time between bookings to avoid overlap. If an appointment conflicts with this gap it will be rejected when creating or updating.

Auto-Complete

If an appointment passes and is still "Scheduled", Trimlyt can automatically mark it as Finished, Canceled, or No Show based on your preference. This helps keep your records up-to-date.
