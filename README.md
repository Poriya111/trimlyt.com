# Trimlyt
https://trimlyt.com 

Trimlyt is a simple, intuitive booking and revenue management tool for barbers. Designed to reduce no-shows, track earnings, and simplify appointment management — all in one place.

## Features

- **Appointment Management:** Add, edit, and track client bookings easily.  
- **No-Show Reduction:** Automated reminders and confirmation requests to reduce missed appointments.  
- **Revenue Overview:** See total revenue, paid/unpaid appointments, and profits at a glance.  
- **Google Calendar Sync:** Keep your schedule synchronized across devices.  
- **Easy Onboarding:** Set up your account and start managing appointments in minutes.

## How to Use

1. Sign up for a free account at [trimlyt.com](https://trimlyt.com).  
2. Connect your Google Calendar (optional but recommended).  
3. Add your services and upcoming appointments.  
4. Track revenue, payments, and appointment confirmations in real time.  
5. Enjoy fewer no-shows and better business visibility.

## Who is it for?

Trimlyt is built specifically for barbers who want to save time, reduce missed appointments, and get clear insights into their income.
